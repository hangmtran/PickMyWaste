About

Help reduce food waste by using Pick My Waste. Pick My Waste addresses food waste in the restaurant and retail industries. Instead of throwing away unsellable but still edible items, contribute to Pick My Waste.

Pick My Waste brings awareness to all the available food in the area. Whether you are a local community organization distributing food, or the general public, we provide the resources to see what is available within your community.
